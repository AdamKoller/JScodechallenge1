Code Challenge 1

Adam went to three different restuarants. The bills were $104,$47, and $228. He decides the tip under 3 conditions:

- 20% if bill is less than $50
- 15% if bill is between $50-$200
- 10% if bill is greater than $200

Create: 1) an array with all three tip values for the given bill 2) an array with all three FINAL amounts(bill+tip)
